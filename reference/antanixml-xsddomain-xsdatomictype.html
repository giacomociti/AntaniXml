AntaniXml

XsdAtomicType

Namespace: AntaniXml
Parent Module: XsdDomain

Enumeration of the built-in types defined by W3C XML Schema. A few ones are not supported: Notation, NmTokens, Id, Idref, Idrefs, Entity and Entities.
